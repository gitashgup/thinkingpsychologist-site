About Ashwarya

A rare combination of enterprise credibility and behavioural depth.

Ashwarya Gupta is an AI transformation leader, facilitator, speaker, and host of the Thinking Psychologist podcast. With 16+ years of global experience spanning innovation, product strategy, automation, and enterprise systems, she has built her career at the intersection of technology, business value, and human behaviour.
